Kimchijjigae

Description

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Suger
  • Kimchi
  • Leet
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Put the olive oil and boil with kimchi
  2. Input the water with garlic, suger, pork and boil for 15 mins
  3. Put 2 spoons of soy sauce
  4. put leet and boil 2~3 more minutes
